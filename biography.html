BIOGRAPHY

Gino Wong (b. 2000) is a Chinese multimedia artist based in London and Guangzhou. His works have been exhibited internationally in China and UK and held in collections including  HUAWEI . With a Master’s Degree in Fine Art Printmaking and a background of media study, his works concern the distortion and inauthenticity brought by the mass media in the modern era and attempt to rebuild a reciprocal relation between the human body and the living world, creating works in printmaking, painting, performance, and writing,

​

He works with printmaking performance, video, installation, and writing.
