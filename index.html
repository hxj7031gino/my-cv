Artist Statement

My work explores the tension between emptiness and presence, using minimal forms to evoke a sense of quiet contemplation. Each piece is an invitation to pause, reflect, and engage with the subtle interplay of light, space, and material.

Biography

Gino Wong (b. 1996, Hong Kong) is a contemporary artist whose practice spans painting, installation, and conceptual art. Educated at the Hong Kong Academy of Arts, Wong’s work has been exhibited internationally and recognized for its meditative qualities.
