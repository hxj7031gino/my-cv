Xuanjun Huang (Gino)

Artist Statement Work Biography

My practice concerns the power relation of image, gender and social distribution, and existentialism. Significant debates of such are being destructed by the dominating but entertaining and fragmented communication technology. I attempt to subvert the present way of image and information communication and empower images the capacity of “de-spectacularization” with a revolutionary, being-in-the-world way of seeing.
