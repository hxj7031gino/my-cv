Untitled 2023

Everything is destiny
Fickle, swift

Whoever says You
He has nothing
Whoever says It
He possesses everything

Whoever says I-You
It is the bridging of We
Whoever says I-It
It is a puppet in a play

Discerning Thou midst a million It
Basking in the mellow sun in autumn

There is pale and solemn
And genial gaze

It   is the dry wind in autumn
You   are the warm sunlight outside window

Bitter days linger
Darkness rises sooner
The war will end, as spring confronts
